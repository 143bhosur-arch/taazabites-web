Frequently Asked Questions

How does Taazabites ensure meal freshness?

We prepare all meals daily using locally sourced ingredients and deliver in temperature-controlled packaging to maintain freshness and nutritional value.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes! We offer vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and keto options. Please specify your requirements when ordering.

What areas in Bangalore do you deliver to?

We currently deliver to all major areas of Bangalore including Whitefield, Indiranagar, Koramangala, HSR Layout, and surrounding regions.

How far in advance should I place my order?

We recommend ordering at least 4 hours in advance for same-day delivery. For larger orders or catering, please order 24 hours ahead.
